Our Heritage

Aprameya Enterprises

At Aprameya Enterprises, we are dedicated to delivering excellence in the manufacturing of high-performance fusing machines. With over 30 years of industry experience, we have built a strong reputation as a trusted partner for businesses in the garment and textile sector.

Our journey is rooted in a deep understanding of industry needs, allowing us to design and manufacture machines that offer durability, efficiency, and consistent performance. We continuously evolve with changing technologies to provide solutions that enhance productivity while maintaining superior finishing quality.

Our strength lies in combining advanced engineering with practical functionality. Every machine we produce undergoes strict quality checks to ensure reliability and long-term performance, making us a dependable choice for our customers.

At Aprameya Enterprises, we don't just build machines - we build lasting relationships. Our commitment to quality, affordability, and prompt service has helped us earn the trust of clients across the industry.

Our Journey

Timeline & Milestones

1996

Foundation & Inception

Aprameya Enterprises was established in Bengaluru, Karnataka, starting as a specialist fabrication partner for general textile tooling and support frames.

2002

Hoshidha Brand Launch

Introduced the "Hoshidha" machinery line, starting with our first manual flat-bed fusing press, focusing on robust structural frames.

2008

First 500 Installations

Celebrated our 500th operational machine. Established service engineers directly in major apparel hubs including Tirupur and Coimbatore.

2015

Digitalization & Safety Automation

Integrated state-of-the-art digital temperature sensors, electronic belt alignment modules, and automatic cooldown timers.

2021

Plant Expansion & Custom Sizing

Opened a larger fabrication unit to support the assembly of ultra-wide 1000mm double-heater fusing configurations and automatic loaders.

2026

30+ Years of Excellence

Currently supporting over 1000+ operational machines across India, serving leading clothing manufacturers and export apparel houses.
